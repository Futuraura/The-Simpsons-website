Welcome to The Simpsons Page!

The Simpsons is an American animated sitcom created by Matt Groening. The series first aired in 1989 and has since become one of the longest-running TV shows in history. It follows the lives of the Simpson family — Homer, Marge, Bart, Lisa, and Maggie — in the fictional town of Springfield. Known for its satirical take on American culture, politics, and society, The Simpsons has won dozens of awards and remains a cultural icon.
